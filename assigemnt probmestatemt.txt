The problem statement and assignment goal can be summarized as follows:

1. The Core Problem Statement
Customer support departments in e-commerce companies handle thousands of refund requests daily. These requests are governed by complex, multi-tiered policies (e.g., return windows, customer tiers, digital goods exclusions, fraud flags, and high-value approvals).

Traditional support systems are either:

Too rigid: Simple rule-based bots that cannot handle conversational nuances or complex multi-step user requests.
Too risky: LLM agents that hallucinate, bypass company policies, or promise refunds without actually executing the database transaction.
2. What the Assignment is For
The goal of this assignment is to build and demonstrate a production-grade AI Customer Support Agent (RefundAI) that solves this problem by combining:

Empathetic & Flexible Conversation: Using a Large Language Model (Cohere Command R+) to converse naturally with customers.
Strict Policy Compliance: Forcing the LLM to route every decision through a rigorous multi-step eligibility check (CRM lookup $\rightarrow$ order confirmation $\rightarrow$ eligibility check $\rightarrow$ database action tool).
Reasoning Transparency (Auditability): Providing a real-time admin dashboard where team managers can inspect the step-by-step tool calls, results, and thought processes of the agent.
Voice Interaction (Accessibility): Letting users speak their refund request (STT) and reading the agent's response back to them (TTS).
